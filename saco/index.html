The plan
Top line

Charge

  • Supply Logan with the tools, system, ability and guidance to become SACO's AI center of excellence with a new CoS partner and a new lens on the business and operations.
  • In parallel, support the capacity of the CEO and CFO to leverage AI in their everyday workflows, while giving them the capacity to inspire and permeate an AI forward culture through the organization.

Predicted outcomes

  • Executives become proficient and effective with AI through fluency applied to every day problems.
  • Logan becomes the AI center of excellence for SACO and is able to (1) surface opportunities for cost savings or new value creation, (2) scope and drive those projects with confidence, (3) develop a strong point of view on how and where AI can be used most effectively at SACO.
  • We uncover at least $100,000 in annual value opportunity that is currently unrecognized.
  • Land on a specific framework to deliver and scale AI adoption throughout SACO.
Context
The opportunity

SACO is AI curious: you've seen examples of savant mode, and you're wondering how else you can drive value with this technology.

At the same time, your organization is trending older, and the expertise that runs it lives in the heads of a handful of senior leaders. When they retire, that knowledge walks out the door. That is not a productivity problem. It is a continuity problem with a clock on it.

Two things landed in our conversation. First, the Chief of Staff frame: AI that runs the system for an executive, not a chatbot the executive operates. Second, a knowledge system that starts small, grows, and maintains itself over time, building a foundation for humans and machines to build value in tandem.

The strategic point: AI growth is bottom up. The personal win produces the institutional asset as a byproduct. Every file the executive's Chief of Staff writes is also the start of a knowledge base your next generation can read and your AI can use.

Constraints

You don't want a huge install. No BDO decks, or consulting deep dives that don't compound. You're not looking to completely overhaul old systems, or streamline people.

You don't want to be taken way outside your capability set or be sucked down endless "AI automation" rabbit holes.

You want to be brought up to speed, empowered and turned loose.

What you get
Scope of work
Component 1
Onboard the Chief of Staff

A complete working Chief of Staff system for Logan, enabling him to access, analyze, scope opportunity across the organization.

What this takes:

  • Pre-flight discovery and an apps-and-connections intake that maps where the work actually lives.
  • Setup of Claude Cowork
  • Set up knowledge vault: AI's read into how the executive thinks, what good looks like, the hard lines, plus company context and current priorities.
  • Connected workspace: cloud storage, and the mail, calendar, and task systems the executive actually uses.
  • The operating rules, skills and learning loop: the pre-built workflows that allow AI to learn and be useful.
  • Seeded with existing documents so it produces real work on day one.
  • Operating cheat sheet and handoff.
Component 2
Operational and scaffold support

This is a new way of working. The install is the start, not the finish. Over the engagement we drive the system against surfacing growth opportunities and driving AI assisted value across the org.

  • Weekly working sessions focused on the executive's chosen objective.
  • Async support hotline built into Teams: you ask a question, I triage and respond. For system tuning between sessions, anytime, anywhere, M-F 8am–5pm.
  • Ad hoc impromptu in-person working sessions.
  • Live learning dashboard (logs learnings, issues, opportunities and insights). Used to update the CoS system, and visible to keep execs or the entire company updated and to drive curiosity.
Component 3
1:1 executive AI fluency coaching — two executives

Deep focus on infusing AI into the CEO and CFO's workflows. AI-aware to every day AI-user for executive level problems. Be able to drive use and inspiration down through the company through your own actions.

  • Personalized coaching meeting you where you're at, within your AI circle of competence.*
  • Kickoff and set up basics session (2 hours).*
  • One session per week per executive focused on real work.
  • Bi-weekly group session: focused on system and bringing the work together.
  • Async support "hotline" built into Teams. You ask a question, I triage and respond.
  • Additional learning modules and resources as needed.
  • Live learning dashboard: record and surface what lessons are extensible to the organization, and how we can drive learning and use down the chain.

*Built on the Rejoin Executive AI Learning Model.

Principles
How we work
  • Progress tracking: Live dashboards updated weekly. These are meant to surface real discussion and feedback on value outcomes and guide the next phase of work. Adjusting on the fly.
  • Communication: Phone, email, zoom, chat, in person all work. I build a dedicated hotline through Teams where you can dump questions for async triage by me.
  • Agreement cancelable at any time. If you're not happy, we stop.
  • You keep everything. All processes, artifacts, deliverables, code, scripts and install.
The proof
Why this works

How much value is laying around because you haven't seen it, had an idea but forgot about it, or couldn't find a quick path to investigate it?
